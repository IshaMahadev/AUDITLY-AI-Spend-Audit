**FAQ:**

**Q: Do I need to connect my AWS or Stripe billing?**
A: No. We know you don't want to give a random tool API access to your billing. You manually input your seat counts and plans.

**Q: How does Credex make money on a free tool?**
A: We sell discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your audit shows massive overspend, we'll offer to source those exact tools for you at a discount. If you're perfectly optimized, we'll tell you that too.

**Q: Are my inputs saved or sold?**
A: All audit math is executed instantly on your device. We only save the final JSON report to our database if you explicitly request a shareable link to send to your team. 

**Q: Why do you need my team size?**
A: Vendors like OpenAI and Anthropic have strict minimums on their "Team" plans. If you have 3 developers, paying for a 5-seat minimum plan is burning cash. We calculate those thresholds automatically.

**Q: Can I share the report with my Head of Finance?**
A: Yes. At the end of the audit, you can generate an anonymized public link to drop into Slack.
