# Unit Economics

## The Value of a Converted Lead
To understand the economics of this tool, we must first determine what a "converted lead" is worth to Credex.
- Let's assume an average Series A startup has an annual AI spend of $60,000 across APIs and seats.
- Credex provides a blended discount of 15% on this infrastructure ($9,000 savings for the startup).
- Credex takes a 30% cut of the generated savings as their margin.
- **LTV of one converted startup:** $2,700/year.

## CAC at Each Channel
From the GTM plan:
1. **Cold Emailing CFOs:** 
   - Cost of Apollo/LinkedIn Sales Nav: ~$100/mo.
   - 500 emails sent -> 10% click rate -> 50 audits -> 5 leads -> 1 conversion.
   - **CAC:** ~$100.
2. **Hacker News / Reddit (Organic):**
   - Cost: Time (roughly 5 hours of founder time). 
   - 1000 visitors -> 300 audits -> 30 leads -> 3 conversions.
   - **CAC:** $0 direct cost.
3. **Credex Internal Email List:**
   - Cost: $0 (owned channel).
   - **CAC:** $0.

## Required Conversion Rates for Profitability
If our blended CAC is $50 and our LTV is $2,700, this tool is insanely profitable from day one, *provided the funnel holds up*.
Let's define the minimum viable funnel to remain profitable against engineering maintenance costs (~$2k/mo for hosting and maintenance time):
- **Audits Completed to Lead Capture:** We need 20% of people who see the final screen to enter their email to save it.
- **Lead to Consultation:** Of those leads, we need 10% to book a Credex consultation.
- **Consultation to Closed Deal:** We need a 20% close rate on the calls.

To generate 1 closed deal (value $2,700), we need:
- 5 consultations booked
- 50 emails captured
- 250 audits completed

## Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months
To generate $1,000,000 ARR, Credex needs **370 paying customers** (at $2,700 LTV).
What has to be true over 18 months?
- We need to close ~20 customers per month.
- Using the funnel math above, we need 20 * 250 = **5,000 completed audits per month**.
- At a 30% landing page conversion rate, we need **16,666 unique visitors per month**.

To achieve ~16k visitors/mo, organic social and Hacker News will not be enough. We would need to:
1. Turn the shareable URL feature into a viral loop (e.g., adding "Compare your spend against your peers").
2. Deploy paid search ads against high-intent keywords like *"GitHub Copilot vs Cursor cost"* or *"Claude Team plan pricing"*. Since our LTV is $2,700, we can easily afford a $200 CPA on search ads.
